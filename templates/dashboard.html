STAY INFORMED

Tips for media literacy

  • Always read beyond the headline before forming an opinion.
  • Cross-check important news with two independent outlets.
  • Be cautious of stories that trigger strong emotional reactions.
  • Check the publish date — old stories often resurface misleadingly.
  • Look for named sources. Anonymous "insiders" are a red flag.
